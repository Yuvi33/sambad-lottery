About Us

Welcome to Lottery Sambad, your number one source for daily lottery results in India. We are dedicated to providing you with the fastest and most accurate updates for the Nagaland State Lottery, Kerala State Lottery, and Sikkim State Lotteries.

Founded in 2026, Lottery Sambad has come a long way from its beginnings. We recognized the need for a reliable, mobile-friendly platform where players could easily check their 1 PM, 6 PM, and 8 PM results without clutter and pop-ups. That's when we decided to build this platform.

Our team works around the clock to ensure that results are fetched and published the moment the official draws are completed. We also provide archives of old results so you can check historical data at any time.

We hope you enjoy our website as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to reach out to us via our Contact Us page.
